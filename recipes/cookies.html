Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic recipe anyone should try making at some point! This recipe is straightforward and easy for anyone to follow. The cookies you make from this recipe will be nice and chewy on the inside, covered in melty chocolate chips, and a little crispy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White and Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Hot Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars together
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  3. Dissolve baking soda into hot water and add to mixture
  4. Add flour and chocolate chips, then stir
  5. Place the dough on baking sheet
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  7. Bake dough in oven for around 10 minutes or until the edges are golden rbwon
