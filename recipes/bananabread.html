Banana Bread

Photo by Jeff Siepman on Unsplash

Description

Banana bread is a popular snack or dessert. Moist, packed with banana flavor, and delicious. Banana bread prefers to use overripe bananas in order to have a stronger flavor. Not only this, overripe bananas will also result in a sweeter flavor.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Overripe Bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix butter and brown sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Mash the bananas and mix them in eggs.
  4. Whisk the flour, salt, and baking soda together. Then add them to mixture.
  5. Add to pan, then bake in the oven for around an hour

You can check when the bread is ready by using a toothpick. Insert a toothpick into the center of the loaf. If there's batter on it, then the bread needs more time. If it's clean, then the bread is ready.
